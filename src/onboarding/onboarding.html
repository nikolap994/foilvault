Welcome to FoilVault

A local-first, zero-knowledge credential vault. Your passwords never leave your device.

How your vault is protected

🔑

AES-GCM 256-bit encryption

Every credential is encrypted before it is written to browser storage. Without your master password, the ciphertext is meaningless.

🏠

Local-first — no cloud

Your vault lives in chrome.storage.local on your device. Nothing is transmitted to any server. Not even FoilVault's developers can see your data.

🧠

Zero-knowledge key derivation

Your master password is run through PBKDF2-SHA256 at 600,000 iterations to derive the vault key. The password itself is never stored — only the encrypted data.

⏱

Auto-lock on idle

The vault locks automatically after 15 minutes of browser inactivity. The derived key is cleared from memory. Unlock requires re-entering your master password.

Your first time using FoilVault

  1. 1

    Create your vault

    Click the FoilVault icon in your browser toolbar. You will be prompted to choose a master password. Choose something strong — this is the only key that unlocks your vault.

    Your master password cannot be recovered if forgotten. Write it down and store it securely offline before creating the vault.
  2. 2

    Add your first credential

    Tap + in the unlocked view. Fill in the site name, username, and password. FoilVault shows you a real-time strength score and entropy estimate for the password field.

  3. 3

    Autofill on login pages

    When you visit a login page for a site in your vault, FoilVault's content script detects the form fields and offers to fill them. The vault must be unlocked for autofill to work.

    Autofill checks the site against FoilGuard's risk score. If the domain looks like an impersonation, autofill is blocked and you see a warning — protecting you from phishing even if you navigate to a spoofed URL.
  4. 4

    Import from another manager

    Already using a password manager? Click the import icon (⬆) in the vault header. FoilVault accepts Bitwarden JSON exports and Chrome passwords CSV exports.

Advanced features

TOTP
Two-factor codes built in

Store your TOTP secret in any credential entry. FoilVault generates and copies the 6-digit code directly from the popup — no separate authenticator app needed.

BREACH
Have I Been Pwned check

For any stored password, tap "Check for breaches" to query the HIBP API using k-anonymity — your full password is never sent.

GEN
Password generator

Generate strong passwords up to 64 characters with configurable character sets. The generator shows entropy in bits so you can see exactly how strong the password is.

EXPORT
JSON and CSV export

Export your entire vault as JSON or CSV for offline backup. The export is plaintext — store it encrypted, not in a cloud drive.
